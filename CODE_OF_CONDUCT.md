# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Be direct, respectful, and focused on the work. Disagreement is welcome when it
improves correctness, maintainability, or user safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Repeated off-topic disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Bad-faith issue spam or intentionally misleading reports

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and may restrict
participation for behavior that harms the project.
